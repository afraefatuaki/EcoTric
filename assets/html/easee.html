Easee Home

Easee Home is intelligent and adapts to all electric vehicles, power supplies and mains. No need to think about technical specifications – the Charging Robot will automatically detect what power the vehicle requires and manages to charge several cars with different needs at the same time.
• Full of power, the charger can charge on both 1 and 3 phase power and supports charging up to 22 kW.
• Gives you full control over your chargers whether at home or on the go.
• Type 2 connector, 3 phases and a maximum current of 32A.
• Solid steel post and scratch-resistant powder coating.
• Fast charging,you can charge up to 10x* times faster than with a regular outlet.
